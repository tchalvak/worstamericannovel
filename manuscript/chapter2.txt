## Truth hurts

But first, let's talk about **truth**.  Just like everyone else, we'll talk about truth like it's some *absolute*, as if anyone knows a damned thing in this world.  But at least we'll preface that:  **Truth** is bullshit.


Alex made her way through life avoiding people and events that believed in "Truth".  Her father and Uncle had believed in "Truth", and their truth had hurt.
